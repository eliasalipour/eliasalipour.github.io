Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy outlines how the "Jalali Toolkit for Sheets" add-on handles your information.

Information Collection and Use

The "Jalali Toolkit for Sheets" add-on does not collect, track, store, or share any personal information or data from your Google account, your Google Sheets, your documents, or your device.

Permissions

All permissions requested by this add-on during installation are strictly necessary for its core functionality. This includes permissions to create a menu, open a sidebar, and insert/read date values in the active spreadsheet you are working on. This data is processed in real-time and is never stored or transmitted anywhere.

Changes to This Policy

Any future changes to this privacy policy will be updated on this page.
